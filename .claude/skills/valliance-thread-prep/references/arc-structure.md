# Valliance Thread Arc Structure

This file contains the structural reference data for each arc in the Valliance Thread curriculum. The skill uses this to determine which PMF canvas anchors, questions, and reading lists apply to the current week.

## The Cycle

One full cycle is 12 weeks. Six arcs, two weeks each.

| Weeks | Arc | Focus |
|-------|-----|-------|
| 1-2 | Arc 1: Agentic AI | Why enterprises fail at agentic deployment. What Valliance believes about building AI that works. |
| 3-4 | Arc 2: Palantir / Ontology | Why business logic is the hidden layer. What Valliance brings to ontology-led transformation. |
| 5-6 | Arc 3: People Enablement | Why adoption fails at the people layer. What Valliance believes about building capability, not compliance. |
| 7-8 | Arc 4: The Consulting Craft | Why the traditional consulting model is broken. What it means to be a Valliance consultant. |
| 9-10 | Arc 5: Agentic Engineering | How to build in the new world. The practitioner layer: architectures, tooling, evaluation, delivery. |
| 11-12 | Arc 6: Value Realisation | How to prove and sustain AI value. Making the outcome-based model work in practice. |

## Week Types

| Week | Type | Purpose |
|------|------|---------|
| 1 (odd weeks) | Problem + Landscape | What is broken? How are others approaching it? Includes mandatory Steelman. |
| 2 (even weeks) | Position + Pitch | What does Valliance believe and why? Deliver it under pressure. Includes Anti-Position, Cross-Arc Bridge, Ghost Prospect. |

## Arc Detail

### Arc 1: Agentic AI

**PMF Canvas Anchors:**
- Problem: AI Inflection Point (business model transformation), Governance & Visibility (human-agent control gap), Business Logic Layer (hidden enterprise rules)
- Solution: Build AI That Works, Holistic Enterprise Transformation (agentic systems pillar), AI Enabled Value Acceleration

**Search terms for bookmarks:** agentic AI, AI agents, enterprise agents, agent governance, agent autonomy, agent orchestration, human-agent collaboration, agent safety, agent deployment, production agents

**Week 1 Problem Question:** Why do enterprise agentic AI deployments fail, and what are the structural reasons beyond "it's early"?

**Week 1 Landscape Question:** How are Palantir, Anthropic, OpenAI, AWS, and the traditional SIs each approaching agentic AI, and where does each model break down?

**Week 2 Position Question:** What does Valliance specifically believe about building agentic AI in the enterprise, and why should a CTO trust us over the alternatives?

**Landscape players to map:** Palantir AIP, Anthropic (Claude/MCP/Cowork), OpenAI (Codex/Assistants), AWS (Bedrock Agents), Traditional SIs (Accenture, Deloitte), DIY/open-source (LangGraph, LangChain)

### Arc 2: Palantir / Ontology

**PMF Canvas Anchors:**
- Problem: Business Logic Layer (hidden enterprise rules), AI Inflection Point (need for ontological coherence)
- Solution: Holistic Enterprise Transformation (enterprise ontology/twin pillar), Build AI That Works (data platforms, ontologies)
- Existing Alternatives: Silicon Valley Tech (Palantir: fast IP, low intimacy), System Integrators (scale without transformation)

**Search terms for bookmarks:** Palantir, ontology, knowledge graph, data fabric, semantic layer, business logic, enterprise data, data platform, digital twin, Foundry

**Week 3 Problem Question:** Why is the business logic layer the hidden bottleneck in enterprise AI, and why do most organisations not even know it exists?

**Week 3 Landscape Question:** How does Palantir's ontology-first approach compare to knowledge graphs, data fabrics, semantic layers, RAG, and "build it ourselves"?

**Week 4 Position Question:** What is Valliance's position on ontology-led enterprise AI, how does our Palantir partnership fit, and what do we bring that Palantir alone does not?

**Landscape players to map:** Palantir Foundry, Neo4j/graph databases, Databricks Unity Catalog, Informatica, dbt/semantic layers, RAG-only approaches, internal "shadow AI" teams

### Arc 3: People Enablement

**PMF Canvas Anchors:**
- Problem: AI Inflection Point (enterprises that become AI-native need people transformation), Governance & Visibility (human-agent collaboration requires new skills)
- Solution: People Lead / AI Enablement, Scale The Impact (change, education/enablement), Set The Goal (education, vision, conviction)

**Search terms for bookmarks:** AI enablement, AI adoption, AI training, change management, workforce transformation, skills gap, AI literacy, leadership AI, employee AI, adoption plateau

**Week 5 Problem Question:** Why does enterprise AI adoption fail at the people layer, and why is "training" the wrong word for what's needed?

**Week 5 Landscape Question:** How are enterprises, consultancies, and technology vendors approaching AI enablement today, and why do most programmes fail to change behaviour?

**Week 6 Position Question:** What does Valliance believe about people-led AI enablement, and how does our approach produce capability rather than compliance?

**Landscape players to map:** Microsoft Copilot adoption programme, Accenture change management, McKinsey AI enablement, Google Gemini Enterprise, LinkedIn Learning, internal AI champions networks

### Arc 4: The Consulting Craft

**PMF Canvas Anchors:**
- Solution: Value-Aligned Commercial Model (outcome-based pricing), New Consulting Model for the Future Enterprise, AI Enabled Value Acceleration (value loops)
- Existing Alternatives: Silicon Valley Tech (fast IP, low intimacy), System Integrators (scale without transformation)

**Search terms for bookmarks:** consulting model, consulting AI, outcome-based pricing, value-based pricing, time and materials, consulting disruption, AI consultancy, professional services, day rate, consulting transformation

**Week 7 Problem Question:** Why is the traditional consulting model broken in the age of AI, and what specific behaviours does it produce that damage client outcomes?

**Week 7 Landscape Question:** How are different consulting models evolving in response to AI, and what are the emerging commercial structures that could replace time-and-materials?

**Week 8 Position Question:** What does it mean to be a Valliance consultant, and how does our commercial model create better outcomes than the alternatives?

**Landscape players to map:** Accenture/Deloitte (traditional SI), Operand/boutique AI consultancies, OpenAI consulting arm, AWS Professional Services, WPP (outcome-based pivot)

### Arc 5: Agentic Engineering

**PMF Canvas Anchors:**
- This arc is practitioner-focused. It covers how Valliance builds, not what Valliance sells.

**Search terms for bookmarks:** agent architecture, agent framework, MCP, agent evaluation, evals, agent orchestration, LangGraph, agent observability, production agents, agent debugging, agent testing, code generation, vibe coding

**Week 9 Problem Question:** What are the hardest unsolved problems in building production-grade agentic systems for the enterprise, and where does the current tooling fall short?

**Week 9 Landscape Question:** What are the emerging architecture patterns, frameworks, and toolchains for building enterprise agentic systems?

**Week 10 Position Question:** How does Valliance engineer agentic systems, and what are our non-negotiable technical principles for enterprise-grade builds?

**Landscape players to map:** Palantir AIP/Foundry, Anthropic Claude/MCP, OpenAI Codex/Assistants, LangGraph/LangChain, AWS Bedrock Agents, Azure AI Agent Service, custom architectures

### Arc 6: Value Realisation

**PMF Canvas Anchors:**
- Solution: Scale The Impact (value realisation, AI ops, governance), Value-Aligned Commercial Model (ROI measurement, outcome-linked pricing), AI Enabled Value Acceleration (compound learning across cycles)

**Search terms for bookmarks:** AI ROI, AI value, value measurement, outcome-based, AI business case, AI investment, value engineering, AI metrics, compound value, value realisation

**Week 11 Problem Question:** Why do most enterprises struggle to prove AI ROI, and why is the measurement problem harder than it looks?

**Week 11 Landscape Question:** How are enterprises, consultancies, and vendors currently measuring and proving AI value, and where does each approach break down?

**Week 12 Position Question:** How does Valliance measure and prove AI value, and how does our value loop model create a structurally better answer than the alternatives?

**Landscape players to map:** McKinsey value driver trees, Accenture value engineering, Microsoft Copilot Dashboard, Palantir value engineering, emerging AI-native measurement approaches
